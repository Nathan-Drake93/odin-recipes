Creamy Tuscan Chicken

Description

This creamy gluten free Tuscan chicken is a comforting one-pan meal that brings together tender, golden seared chicken cutlets, sun-dried tomatoes, fresh spinach, and a rich parmesan cream sauce. The pasta cooks directly in the pan, soaking up all the savory flavors for a dish that's both hearty and simple to make. Perfect for a cozy weeknight dinner or a meal that feels restaurant worthy without the fuss.

Ingredients

  • 3 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 Cup Gluten Free Flour
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 4 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 2 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 1/2 Cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/3 Cup Sun-Dried Tomatoes
  • 2 Cups Baby Spinach
  • 1 Tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 9 Oz Gluten Free Tagliatelle or Fettuccine (I use Jovial)
  • Red Pepper Flakes (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Prep chicken. Season cutlets with salt and pepper. Dredge in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chicken and cook 4-5 min per side until golden. Set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add garlic and saut√© 30 seconds.
  5. Add broth and deglaze the pan.
  6. Pour in heavy cream, add Italian seasoning, and sun-dried tomatoes and stir.
  7. Add in pasta, bring to a boil, cover, and cook until preferred tenderness. Stirring occasionally.
  8. Add parmesan and simmer 2-3 min until slightly thickened.
  9. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted.
  10. Stir the chicken back into the pan, and simmer 2-3 minutes more.
  11. Garnish with red pepper flakes if using. Serve immediately.

Notes

  • If you want to use a corn based noodle like that of Barilla, use 12 oz. Corn based noodles absorb less liquid than rice based pasta.
  • For a simpler option, dice the chicken and skip the flour dredge. It will still taste great with less of a mess to clean up.
